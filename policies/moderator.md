# Web of Things (WoT) Policy (Draft)
## Task Force Moderators
This is the WoT policy document governing Task Force Moderators.
### Selection Process
- W3C process for selection
The WoT Working Group shall follow the W3C process for task force moderator selection, with the following considerations.
- Chairs role
- Task force members role
#### Nomination process and times
A Task Force moderator nomination may be proposed at any time by any member of the WoT Working Group. An discussion item shall be added to the task force agenda, and the task force shall discuss the nomination and arrive at a consensus for the nomination to proceed. The task force shall consider the nomination and, after a two week decision period, pass a resolution to accept the nomination. After passing the resolution, the nomination shall be forwarded to the Working Group for approval. 
### Duties and Responsibilities
- Hold coordination meetings as needed
- Organize the Task Force meetings with an agenda
- Moderate the Task Force meetings
- Review the minutes from the meetings
- Manage toward deliverables
### Multiple moderators
- A task force may have more than one moderator
- Produce a written statement of the division of responsibilities
